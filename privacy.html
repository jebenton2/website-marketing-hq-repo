Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They contain information about your browsing activity and preferences. Cookies help us remember your information, understand how you use our website, and improve your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how you use our website, such as which pages you visit and how long you stay on them. This helps us understand user behavior and improve our services.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and choices to provide a more personalized experience when you return to our website.
  • Marketing Cookies: These cookies track your activity across websites to display targeted advertisements and marketing content relevant to your interests.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to accept or reject cookies, or be notified when a cookie is being set. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your user experience.

You can also opt out of targeted advertising by adjusting your privacy settings on social media platforms and advertising networks, or by visiting industry opt-out resources.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers, such as analytics companies and advertising networks, to place cookies on your device. These third parties may collect information about your online activities across different websites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies to understand their data practices.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we limit how we use your information.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of marketing communications and targeted advertising.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact Us section below. We will respond to your request within the timeframe required by applicable law.

Our legal basis for processing your personal information includes:
