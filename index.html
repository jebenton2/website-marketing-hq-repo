About Website Marketing HQ

Building Better Content Strategies

Website Marketing HQ was founded in 2018 by a team of content strategists, SEO experts, and marketing technologists who were frustrated with how fragmented the content creation process had become. We realized that teams were spending more time managing tools and processes than actually creating great content. That's when we decided to build a better solution. Starting with a small group of beta customers, we've grown to serve hundreds of companies across industries, from ambitious startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. Our platform is built on the collective wisdom of our customers and the latest advances in AI and content science.

Our mission is simple: empower marketing teams to create content that drives real business results. We believe that great content starts with strategy, is powered by data, and is executed with precision. We're committed to building tools that respect your team's creativity while amplifying their impact. Every feature we build, every update we release, is guided by our core values: obsessive focus on customer success, relentless commitment to data-driven decisions, and unwavering dedication to making content marketing more accessible and effective for everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our platform and how it can help your team succeed.

How quickly will I see results from using your platform?

Most teams see measurable improvements within 2-4 weeks. With our SEO content briefs, you'll immediately have better-informed content strategy. Within 30 days, you should see improved search rankings and engagement metrics. Our AI-assisted copy features can boost productivity immediately, while the full impact of your optimized content strategy typically compounds over 3-6 months as more content is published and begins ranking. We've seen customers achieve 300% traffic increases within 6 months of implementation.

Can your platform integrate with our existing tools and workflows?

Absolutely! Our platform integrates seamlessly with popular tools including WordPress, HubSpot, Slack, Asana, Monday.com, and many others. We offer both native integrations and API access for custom connections. Our team can help you set up integrations that match your existing workflow, so you don't have to change how you work. We also support importing and exporting data in multiple formats, making it easy to bring in your existing content and export reports for analysis.

Is our content data secure and private on your platform?

Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations. Your content remains your property—we never use it to train AI models or share it with third parties. We perform regular security audits, maintain automated backups, and have a dedicated security team. You can also implement single sign-on (SSO) and control user permissions at a granular level.

What kind of training and support do you provide?

We provide comprehensive onboarding including a personalized strategy session, team training workshops, and detailed documentation. Every customer gets access to our knowledge base with video tutorials and best practice guides. We offer live chat support, email support, and scheduled calls with our success team. For enterprise customers, we assign a dedicated success manager who works with you to maximize ROI. We also host monthly webinars on content strategy, platform features, and industry best practices.

How is your pricing structured, and what's included in each plan?

We offer flexible pricing based on your team size and needs. Our Starter plan is perfect for small teams, including core features like SEO content briefs and basic AI-assisted copy. Our Professional plan adds advanced analytics, custom workflows, and priority support. Our Enterprise plan includes everything plus dedicated support, custom integrations, and advanced security features. We don't charge per piece of content—you get unlimited content production. We offer a 14-day free trial so you can explore all features, and we're happy to create a custom plan for your specific requirements.
